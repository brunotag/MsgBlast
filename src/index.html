The Problem

Reaching out a list of contacts to send a certain message to all of them is hard.

Especially if you want your message to be personalised at some extent.

A typical example is represented by small business owners trying to reach out their customers to propose them offers or deals: a common solution is a Facebook page, but some people don't have Facebook.

Also, posting on the Facebook profile of the business wouldn't help personalisation.

The Solution

A product that would allow to send a certain message (with some degree of personalisation, e.g. the name) to a group of "contacts", through different platforms (Facebook, SMS, LinkedIn, email, ...)

Current State

Alpha: we currently support sending SMSs to a list of messages. Please click on the "Try the alpha!" button on the top of the website.
